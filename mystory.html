Hello There!

Now, you might have doubt about my career in designing world as I am from a IT (hardware and software) background. Here is my journey, I did my bachelor’s in electronics and Tele-communication from St.Vincent Pallotti college, India. From where I got to learn so many subjects in engineering and got interest in robotics and started to learn the development of robotics, also participated in various robotics competitions. Moreover, the very interesting thing which attracted me was designing of bots (hardware and software designing). To take industrial experience in this area, I started my full time internship in “V S informatic’s” one of the finest company in our region. Where I work with embedded wizard to design the UI of the machines (washing machines, robots and many university level projects). After getting this experience I wanted to go deeper in designing and wanted to explore more not just with controller’s but also with daily usable products (web designing and application designing).

I decided to do my Master’s from Germany (SRH Hochschule Heidelberg) because I wanted to gain some international experience in my career and also wanted to jump to designing career. Unfortunately, because of my background with hardware and software technology, I didn’t get admission in designing courses after which I chose to continue with my field. From here, I started my journey to become an UI/UX designer and grasp theoretical knowledge from online learning platforms coursera and udemy, where I found incredible mentor (Daniel Walter Scott ) to work and learn with. I was managing my interest and master studies together, which was challenging for me. After finishing with my theoretical subject’s I did my master’s thesis in “Diehl Controls” as a UI/UX designer, where I worked on mobile application for helping software developer’s in the company for ‘TLS Bootloader’ projects, which is explained in the portfolio in detail. I Also work as a freelancer with the team of 4 people where I perform my UIUX designing part or logo designing of the given project and others perform there specific tasks.

I have my portfolio to showcase my designing skills which is done by using Adobe xd, Adobe photoshop, Adobe illustrator, adobe InDesign tools (want to explore more in illustration and InDesign). If I talk about my coding skills, I have experience in object oriented programming where, C++ was my primary language and have worked with JAVA to learn backend development (just to know how backend works). Moreover, for developing responsive UI’s for website or mobile application I have also my hands on HTML5 and CSS3. I feel if you want to master in something you should fall in love with that thing to spend more time and this is the reason why I wanted to work in this field to work more and to explore as much as I can.

I can definitely say this about myself that, I am a confident personality to explain and express my work, for me small and big firm doesn’t really matter in any case, for me important is to enjoy the work which doesn’t put me in the pressure of deadline. And the second most prominent thing for me is honesty which I got from my Father (he always taught me to be honest in life with everyone no matter what the situation is!).
