Features (must have's)

• User should be able to register for charging point for particular time period.
• User should be able to see all the charging points within the range (0 to 5km).
• User should be able to rent the registered charging point.
• Rest all the information user need for the registration and settings.

Design Process

“ Find the nearest charge point to explore more ”

Research

when we talk about the mobile application we must think about the users compatibility and the current user trends. It should be easy for user to recognize the symbols used in the application (option, location, search keys). I searched about common icons used in other mobile applications.

Now a days, the first ever step is to research and make the rough wireframe directly on the tool, test it and then design the high fidelity version of the same.

Wireframing

As this was my first project, I didn't understand the importance of wireframe. But as I entetred deeply, I understood, why designer's spend their hours and days on wireframing the project. It easily provide the solutions and save lot of time.

Review

This is my first designed project. The idea was taken by developing website (ideasgrab.com) which helps designers to make new projects on new ideas. After finishing with the mockup, I took a test of my app from 3,4 of my friends where I gave them some tasks to reach to a particular destination in which they fail. After 4,5 test I was satisfied with the review from my friends. And I started to design the application and you can see the results.

Design Delivery

Design guidlines:

UX Principles plays very crucial role in design thinking process. Through out designing the app I followed these UX principles specially User touch behavior and Fitt's law.

User Touch Behavior:

• People hold phones in multiple ways, depending on their device, their needs, and their context.
• 75% of users touch the screen only with one thumb.
• Fewer than 50% of user's hold their phones with one hand.
• 36% of users cradle their phone, using their second hand for both greater reach and
• 10% of user's hold their phone in one hand and tap with a finger of the other hand.

Fitt's Law:

The time to acquire a target is a function of the distance to and size of the target. It means the element that need to be focused. I have to make them bigger and closure to natural area.
